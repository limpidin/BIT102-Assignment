Description

So, what exactly is a hobbyist and how can you know if you are one? Well, the answer to that is very simple. Do you have something that you like doing? It can be any sport, studying, programming, yoga or just about anything. If the answer to that question is yes, then congratulations, you are officially a hobbyist! You can now feel free to visit our other pages, including the user profiles, forums and resources.

Benefits?

So, what are the benefits of being a hobbyist? Why should you pick up a few hobbies and do them during your free time? Well, the benefits include:

  • Increased levels of contentment
  • Reduced anxiety and stress
  • Promotes mental health
  • Helps with healthy aging
  • Helps to regulate physiological functions
According to a study done by Fancourt et al. (2020), older adults with depression increased their odds of recovery by 272% just because they had a hobby!
